# Getting Started with Sales OS

## Step 1: Set Up Your Environment

Make sure you have Claude Code installed. If not, visit [claude.ai/code](https://claude.ai/code).

```bash
chmod +x scripts/setup.sh
./scripts/setup.sh
```

## Step 2: Add Your Business Context

Drop your business documents into the `/knowledge-base/` folder. The more you provide, the better your Sales OS performs.

**What to include:**
- Company overview, pitch deck, or one-pager
- Current email outreach templates and campaigns
- LinkedIn DM scripts you're currently using
- Website copy, landing page copy
- Case studies, testimonials, client results
- Any sales playbooks or SOPs you follow
- Performance data: reply rates, open rates, conversion rates

**Don't have all of this?** That's fine. Start with whatever you have — even a paragraph describing your business. Claude will ask for what's missing.

## Step 3: Launch Sales OS

Open Claude in this project directory and say:

> "Go through the Sales OS and tell me what's the next step."

Claude will:
1. Read your knowledge base
2. Ask you a few targeted questions (5-10 minutes)
3. Generate your personalized business context
4. Audit your current LinkedIn and email setup
5. Deliver a prioritized roadmap of what to fix first

## Step 4: Work Through the Phases

Follow the roadmap Claude gives you. The phases build on each other:

1. **Foundation** — Lock in your ICP, positioning, and offer clarity
2. **LinkedIn Profile** — Optimize your profile so it converts visitors
3. **LinkedIn Outreach** — Build DM sequences that book calls
4. **Email Outreach** — Build email campaigns that get replies
5. **Cross-Channel** — Unify LinkedIn + email into one system
6. **Optimization** — Automate and continuously improve

## Step 5: Feed It Data

As you run campaigns, feed performance data back to Claude:

- "My reply rate on the last campaign was 4%. Here are the emails I sent."
- "Here's the DM sequence I ran — 200 sent, 12 replies, 3 calls booked."

Claude will diagnose what's working, what's not, and adjust your system.

## FAQ

**How long does initial setup take?**
30 minutes for the first audit and quick wins. Full system build-out happens over a few sessions.

**Do I need to use specific tools?**
No. Sales OS works with whatever email and LinkedIn tools you already use. It writes the strategy and copy — you execute with your tools.

**Can I use this for my team?**
Yes. Share the repo with your team. Each person can add their own context to the knowledge base.

**What if I only do LinkedIn (no email)?**
Skip Phase 4. The LinkedIn phases work independently.

**What if I only do email (no LinkedIn)?**
Skip Phases 2-3. The email phases work independently.
